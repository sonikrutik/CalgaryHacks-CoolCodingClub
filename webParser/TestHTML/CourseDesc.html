Midterm Exam Delivery Update

Instructors should review the latest recommendations and instructions for delivering midterm exams remotely and check the following announcement regarding exam support.

Students should ensure they review the tipsheet for taking quizzes online. For courses where students are required to use additional tools such as Smart Exam Monitor or Examlock, we recommend reviewing the following guides:

  • Smart Exam Monitor: Using Smart Exam Monitor - Troubleshooting SEM Issues

  • ExamLock: Using ExamLock - Troubleshooting ExamLock Issues

IST eClass support can be reached by emailing eclass@ualberta.ca. Please note that during the exam period we are experiencing a very high volume of support requests. The eClass team is available from 8:30AM - 9:00PM Monday through Friday.
